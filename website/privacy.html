Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to U Funny ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Run Horse Run LLC. We built U Funny to help stand-up comedians track their journey, improve their craft, and connect with other comics.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using U Funny, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address and password (for your account)
  • Display name / stage name
  • Profile photo
  • Comedy bits, set lists, and notes you create
  • Check-in activity at open mics and venues
  • Video clips you upload or YouTube links you share
  • Critiques, reviews, and feedback you give or receive
  • Reports you submit about other users or content
  • Messages and interactions with other users

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data and feature interactions
  • General location (if you grant permission, for finding open mics nearby)
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the U Funny app and its features
  • Personalize your experience (AI Comedy Coach, recommendations)
  • Enable connections between comedians and audience members
  • Send important account and service notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Moderate content and enforce our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
  • Review user reports, investigate policy violations, and take appropriate action including content removal and account suspension
  • Ensure the safety and security of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations
We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

4. Content Moderation & Safety

To maintain a safe community, we may review, filter, or remove user-generated content that violates our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines.

This includes:

  • Automated content filtering to detect objectionable material
  • Reviewing user reports of objectionable content or abusive behavior
  • Monitoring patterns of reported or blocked users
  • Taking enforcement actions including content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination

When you report or block a user, this information is shared with our moderation team for review. We do not share report details with the reported user.

5. Information We Share

We only share your information in limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Your display name, profile, and public activity (like check-ins or shared sets) are visible to other U Funny users based on your privacy settings
  • Service providers: We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication, and AI providers to power the AI Comedy Coach feature
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of our users
  • Law enforcement: We may share information with authorities when we become aware of content that violates the law or poses a threat to safety
  • Business transfers: If U Funny is acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new owner under the same privacy protections

6. Location Data

U Funny may request access to your device's location to help you find nearby open mics and venues. Location access is always optional -- you can use the app without granting it.

We do not track your location in the background. Location is only accessed when you are actively using the open mic finder feature.

7. Videos and Media

Videos and clips you upload are stored securely in our cloud infrastructure. You own your content. We do not use your videos for any purpose other than displaying them within the app as you intend.

We may review uploaded media as part of our content moderation process to ensure compliance with our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

You can delete your uploaded clips at any time from within the app.

8. Data Security

We take security seriously. Your data is protected by:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Secure, encrypted storage via Supabase
  • Row-level security policies that ensure users can only access their own data
  • Regular security reviews and updates

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your comedy bits and set data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Block other users to prevent them from seeing your content or interacting with you
  • Report content or users that violate our guidelines

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app, or email us at the address below.

10. Children's Privacy

U Funny is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

Continued use of U Funny after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
