## Full Description (max 4000 chars — use for both stores)

U Funny is the must-have app for stand-up comedians hitting the open mic circuit.

Whether you're writing your first joke or polishing a tight 15-minute set, U Funny gives you the tools to level up faster.

**AI COMEDY COACH**
Get real feedback on your material from an AI trained on 25+ years of comedy craft. Share a bit, get instant notes on what's working, what's weak, and punch-up suggestions — like having a veteran comedian in your corner 24/7.

**OPEN MIC FINDER**
Discover open mics near you with a real database of venues. Search by day, filter by city, and never miss a night to get on stage.

**LINEUP MANAGEMENT**
Check in to live events with a room code, see the real-time lineup, and know exactly how many comics are ahead of you before you go up.

**BIT MANAGER**
Write, organize, and track all your jokes in one place. Get AI-powered analysis on any bit — structure, punchlines, delivery notes, and a 1–10 rating.

**COMMUNITY FEATURES**
Connect with other comedians, share sets for feedback, and build your crew.

U Funny is built by comedians, for comedians. No fluff, just the tools you actually need to get better and get booked.

Download free. Start writing. Go be funny.
